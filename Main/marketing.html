Marketing Skills

Marketing refers to activities a company undertakes to promote the buying or selling of a product or service. Marketing includes advertising, selling, and delivering products to consumers or other businesses. Some marketing is done by affiliates on behalf of a company.
Professionals who work in a corporation's marketing and promotion departments seek to get the attention of key potential audiences through advertising. Promotions are targeted to certain audiences and may involve celebrity endorsements, catchy phrases or slogans, memorable packaging or graphic designs and overall media exposure


IMPORTANCE OF MARKETING SKILLS:



  1. Marketing Helps in Transfer, Exchange and Movement of Goods: Marketing is very helpful in transfer, exchange and movement of goods. Goods and services are made available to customers through various intermediaries’ viz., wholesalers and retailers etc. Marketing is helpful to both producers and consumers.
  2. Marketing Creates Employment: Marketing is complex mechanism involving many people in one form or the other. The major marketing functions are buying, selling, financing, transport, warehousing, risk bearing and standardisation, etc. In each such function different activities are performed by a large number of individuals and bodies.
  3. Marketing Is Helpful In Development Of An Economy: Adam Smith has remarked that “nothing happens in our country until somebody sells something”. Marketing is the kingpin that sets the economy revolving. The marketing organisation, more scientifically organised, makes the economy strong and stable, the lesser the stress on the marketing function, the weaker will be the economy.
  4. Marketing Acts as a Source of New Ideas: The concept of marketing is a dynamic concept. It has changed altogether with the passage of time. Such changes have far reaching effects on production and distribution. With the rapid change in tastes and preference of people, marketing has to come up with the same. Marketing as an instrument of measurement, gives scope for understanding this new demand pattern and thereby produce and make available the goods accordingly.

IMPROVEMENT OF MARKETING SKILLS

  1. Gain Knowledge of the Latest Marketing Trends: The most important part of a marketer’s job is always updating his/her knowledge. The world is constantly changing and you either follow along or stay behind and lose. There are a lot of new techniques and technologies popping up all the time that you need to keep track of.
  2. Study Successful Marketers from Today’s World: There’s no better way to become a master than to learn from one.Look out for marketing gurus, see what they do, especially what they do differently than others, and apply those techniques to your business strategy.
  3. Become an Expert on Customer Experience Management (CEM): Customer experience management (CEM) is a way to make sure that customers are happy with the products or services they get from your company.Engaging with them and getting their feedback helps you know what they want, so it’s easier to provide exactly what they want and look for.Customer experience management (CEM) is a hot topic right now.A lot of companies want to improve their CEM strategies, and for good reason – it gives them the necessary information to improve your marketing skills and match the customer’s expectations!
  4. Learn How to Create Great Content: So, you have content that’s well written and relevant to your audience. You’re doing all the right things: sharing it on social media, rewriting headlines for better search engine optimization (SEO), linking to other articles on your site to encourage more readership.
